# Coding Assistant Draft

**Introduction**
My name is Xiaoxi Jiang. I’m a university student who spends a lot of time balancing coursework, group projects, and internship prep, so I care a lot about tools that actually save time without making my work sound fake. I like testing new tech, but I also pay attention to whether it is genuinely useful in day-to-day study and work.

One area that interests me is the ecosystem around major Chinese internet companies like Alibaba, Tencent, and ByteDance. These companies shape how people shop, communicate, consume content, and work online, so the problems they solve are large-scale and very practical. I find this space important because it combines business strategy, user behavior, platform governance, and fast-moving technical innovation.

- Generative AI can help product teams quickly analyze large volumes of user feedback, app reviews, and customer service logs to spot recurring complaints or feature requests.
- It can support operations teams by drafting localized marketing copy, push notifications, and in-app content for different user groups, which speeds up campaign testing.
- It can help engineers and analysts summarize internal documents, meeting notes, and technical reports so teams can make faster decisions across large organizations.

## Reflection

**The exact prompt used:**

```text
You are a top-performing university student completing a homework assignment about using a coding assistant.

Please generate content for a Markdown file named "assistant_draft.md".

The content must follow these requirements exactly:

1. Include a short, clear title.

2. Write one short paragraph introducing yourself as a student. The tone should be natural, personal, and realistic (not overly formal or generic).

3. Write one short paragraph describing an industry, function, or problem area you care about. Focus on Chinese internet companies (such as platforms like Alibaba, Tencent, ByteDance, etc.), and discuss why this area is important and interesting to you.

4. Provide a bullet point list with exactly 3 items explaining how generative AI can be useful in this area. Each point should be specific and practical (not vague).

Additional requirements:
- Write in clean Markdown format
- Keep the length concise (not too long)
- Avoid generic AI-style phrases; make it sound like a real student wrote it
- Do NOT include explanations outside the Markdown content
```

**The things AI did well:**
The assistant did well in following the structure closely and keeping the writing concise. It also stayed focused on Chinese internet companies instead of drifting into a broader tech discussion.

**The parts I just revised:**
One thing that needed clarification was whether “repeat this prompt” meant the full prompt or just the main instruction. It also helped to be careful about tone, because student writing can easily sound too polished or too generic. Also, I add my name in introduction part
